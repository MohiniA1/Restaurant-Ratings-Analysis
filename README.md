# Restaurant-Ratings-Analysis
# Project Name
Restaurant Consumer Behavior & Performance Analysis Dashboard

## Purpose / Objective
To analyze restaurant performance, consumer behavior, dining preferences, and satisfaction levels across multiple cities and states, enabling data-driven decisions for restaurant owners and stakeholders.

## Business Problem

1) Restaurant businesses struggle to understand:
2) Customer preferences across cities & states
3) Impact of pricing, parking, alcohol, and smoking policies on ratings
4) Consumer behavior based on occupation, marital status, and budget
5) Which restaurants perform best in food & service

## Goal of the Dashboard

1) Provide a 360° view of restaurant performance and consumer behavior
2) Identify high-performing restaurants and cuisines
3) Analyze factors influencing customer satisfaction
4) Help stakeholders optimize pricing, services, and facilitie

## Tech Stack Used

1) Excel – Initial data exploration
2) Power Query – Data cleaning & transformation
3) SQL – Data extraction & logic building
4) Power BI – Dashboard creation & storytelling
5) DAX – Measures, KPIs, and calculations
6) Data Modeling – Star schema with relationships
7) Data Cleaning – Handling nulls, duplicates, and data standardization


## Key Features & Highlights
### Home Dashboard

Purpose: High-level business overview

##### Key Visuals & Why Used:

1) KPI Cards – Total restaurants, cuisines, consumers, cities, states
Quick business snapshot

2) Bar Charts – Consumers by city & state
Easy regional comparison

3) Stacked Bar Charts – Smoking rates & parking availability
Compare multiple attributes in one view

#### Insights:
1) San Luis Potosi has the highest consumer count

2) Smoking policies vary significantly by city

3) Parking availability impacts customer convenience

 ### Dining Dashboard

Purpose: Analyze dining preferences & pricing impact

##### Key Visuals & Why Used:
1) Donut Charts – Franchise vs Non-Franchise ratings
Ideal for proportion comparison

2) Line & Area Chart – Price levels vs parking
Trend analysis

3) Bar Charts – Preferred cuisines
Identify most popular food categories

#### Insights:

1) Mexican cuisine dominates customer preference
2) Medium-priced restaurants perform best
3) Franchise restaurants show consistent satisfaction levels

### Hospitality Dashboard

Purpose: Study services like alcohol & transportation

##### Key Visuals & Why Used:

1) Donut Chart – Alcohol service types
Distribution of service offerings

2) Bar Chart – Transportation mode by city
Consumer accessibility analysis

3) Tree Map – Smoking policies by state
Space-efficient comparison

#### Insights:

1) Restaurants without alcohol service still receive high ratings
2) Public transport is the most used mode
3) Smoking sections influence customer satisfaction

### Behavior Dashboard

Purpose: Understand consumer lifestyle & spending behavior

##### Key Visuals & Why Used:
1) Stacked Bar Charts – Occupation vs budget
Spending power comparison

2) Bar Charts – Drinking habits by state
Behavioral trends

3) Clustered Bars – Marital status vs smoking & drinking
Side-by-side behavior comparison

Insights:

1) Students prefer medium budgets
2) Employed consumers show higher spending capacity
3) Casual drinkers form the largest segment

### Review Dashboard

Purpose: Identify top-performing restaurants

##### Key Visuals & Why Used:

1) Stacked Bar Charts – Food & service ratings
Compare satisfaction levels

2) Table with Conditional Formatting – Overall ratings
Easy ranking & filtering

Insights:

1) Tortas Locas Hipocampo leads in overall satisfaction
2) Service quality strongly influences repeat visits
3) Unsatisfactory ratings are minimal for top restaurants

## Business Impact

### Enables restaurant owners to:
1) Improve pricing and service strategies
2) Identify expansion-ready locations
3) Optimize facilities like parking & alcohol services

### Helps stakeholders:

1) Understand customer expectations
2) Increase customer satisfaction & retention
3) Drive data-backed operational decisions
